Tribal

Tribal tattoos are bold, often all-black geometric designs with a rich history rooted in the cultures of indigenous peoples around the world. They were traditionally used to mark social status, achievements, and cultural heritage, with variations found in different tribes.

Portrait

A portrait tattoo is a tattoo that replicates the likeness of a person, pet, or fictional character, often focusing on capturing their essence and expressions. It's a form of realism that demands a high level of detail and precision from the artist. The goal is to create a lasting tribute, whether commemorating a loved one, a personal icon, or a work of art.

Script

A script tattoo is a tattoo design primarily composed of text or lettering, like words, phrases, quotes, or even full sentences. It's a popular way to express personal meaning, with variations in style ranging from fine line, traditional, minimalistic, or freehand.
