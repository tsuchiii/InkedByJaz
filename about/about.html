Jasmin Pareja

With roots deeply grounded in the Bay Area and a lifetime surrounded by creativity, Jasmin Pareja is more than just a tattoo artist — she’s a storyteller through skin. Coming from a family of artists, Jasmin’s connection to art has always been personal, emotional, and alive.

For over 20 years, she’s been creating across different mediums, but for the last 8, tattooing has become her chosen path — the one that lets her turn raw human emotion into something lasting. To Jasmin, every tattoo is more than ink. It’s a memory, a healing process, a tribute, or a moment of joy. The connection she forms with her clients is what fuels her — being trusted to mark someone’s body forever is an honor she never takes lightly.

Each piece she creates is filled with intention, grounded in feeling, and brought to life with care. For Jasmin, tattooing isn’t just art — it’s soul work.
